Artificial Intelligence

What is AI?

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is the simulation of human intelligence in machines that are programmed to think, learn, and solve problems. It encompasses a wide range of technologies including machine learning, natural language processing, and robotics.

Benefits of AI for Humanity

AI holds the potential to revolutionize healthcare, improve education access, enhance transportation systems, and accelerate scientific discoveries. By automating repetitive tasks, it allows humans to focus on creative and strategic work.

Additionally, AI can help address global challenges such as climate change, resource management, and disease prevention by analyzing large-scale data efficiently.
